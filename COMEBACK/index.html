The Rules of The Game

  1. 1

    You are all alone

    No one is coming to save you. You have to take yourself out of this.

  2. 2

    Create or be destroyed

    Build something bigger than you or be torn apart by the weight of oblivion

  3. 3

    Dont feed the demon

    It could end up feeding on your soul until there's nothing left but the beast itself

  4. 4

    Work on Soul Alchemy

    Practice the spirit and essence transmutation to refine them into pure gold.
